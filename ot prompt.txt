Server info:

- PvP Enforced with a modified skull system
- 1x Experience
- 0x Skill rate (Custom system for gaining skill points)
- 1x Loot rate
- Gain experience upon PvP kills
- Death penalty extremely low (0.1%)
- Extremely fast respawn rate
- Limited world access (Mainland would be the main consistent hub, with the other islands/continents being available on a rotation)
- Limitless party EXP sharing (Infinite number of party members sharing with 20% exp increase for each member, regardless of Vocation)
- Runes/Ammo/Mana potions/Amulets/Rings with unlimited charges (Will use Soul points to use runes instead)
- No item loss, under ANY circumstances (including White/Red/Black skull)
- Rebalanced Vocation stats (Health/Mana/Skill gain)
- Limited Exiva (Connected to Skull system)
- Spell and rune formulas based on 7.6 system
- Minor spell adjustments and rebalancing
- New spells



Detailed info:

	PvP Enforced/Hardcore, Skull system, PZ Lock and Exiva:
	
		The number of frags (Killing a non-skulled player) any player can do is unlimited.

		Attacking any player outside of party results in 1 minute PZ.
		Killing any player outside of party results in 5 minute PZ.

		Fragging a player results in a White skull (WS). Expires in 5 minutes.
		Fragging 5 players in the span of 1 hour results in a Red skull (RS). Expires in 1 hour.
		Fragging 15 players in the span of 1 hour results in a Black skull (BS). Expires in 24 hours.

		You can Exiva anyone that is nearby you (1 to 100 sqm away).
		White skull can be Exiva'd far away (101 to 274 sqm away)
		Red skull and Black skull can be Exiva'd anywhere (275+ sqm away).
		Guild members can Exiva members of their Guild anywhere.

		PvP kills will grant Experience upon kill.
		Total value gained by a kill will be 50% of total experience lost, divided by number of players involved in the kill
		
		
	Death penalty & loss:
		
		Player loses 0.1% of total Experience upon death.
		Player loses 0.1% of total Skill points upon death.
		Player NEVER loses his items upon death.

	Experience & spawn rate:
		
		1x Experience rate
		Extremely fast respawn rate. (Enough to claim 1 or 2 small rooms in a regular hunting spot and have consistent respawns)
		 
		
	Skill system & replacement for skilling/mana sitting (EXPERIMENTAL):

		This system is still in design development.

		Since there is no gold sink (or purpose to gold whatsoever), the idea was to
		disable the regular skilling system and instead implement a system that would allow you to convert gold into skills.

		The underlying design for skill points is welcoming to this kind of design, so the technical part would not be an issue.
		Biggest issue is resolving the math/formulas since the difference in skill values on the backend is vastly different when
		comparing magic level to the other skills.

		Fishing could stay the same, but then again there is no much point to fishing at all. Perhaps need to design some fishing mechanics?

		Skilling/Training and mana sitting is very important for the health of the server, due to somewhat inflating the active player numbers,
		idling mechanics (which improve player retention) and the social aspect of the server.

		The idea was to create a similar idling mechanic that you could do outside PZ.
		Finding a good hiding spot is a good mechanic that should be encouraged (promotes world exploration),
		but I also aim to encourage players finding these idling/training players to kill in order to gain some sort of reward.
		The social aspect is also a very important piece of this, lots of player just idle while chatting in game-chat.

		This is where the idea of gold pouch comes in.
		The gold pouch/coin purse is an already existing mechanic, but would be repurposed to hold some sort of tokens (or even just gold?).
		Idling/training with this new system would generate a certain amount of tokens that would appear in your gold pouch.
		Upon getting killed, you would lose these tokens and would be lootable in your corpse.
		You would be able to convert these tokens/coins into skill points using the same gold-to-skill system as mentioned before.

		The amount of tokens generated would most likely be proportional to your level/experience.

		Hoping to find a better solution for this, the current idea is somewhat clashing with the design philosophy.


	Runes/Ammo/Manafluids/Amulets/Rings & Soul system:

		Instead of these resources using charges, they will use Soul points.
		Soul points will passively regenerate at the rate of 1 Soul per 2 seconds.
		In some cases, where one Soul is too valuable for the charge of 1 resource (such as just one bolt/arrow for example), they player will use Mana instead.

		These are the values we will be using in the first iteration:
			Resource name: Soul points (Ranges will be used if unsure of value)
			Runes:
			- SD: 7-10
			- UH: 1
			- IH: 1
			- GFB: 3-4 (and all area runes comparable to GFB)
			- Explosion: 3-4 (will need to adjust either Explosion damage formula or Soul cost)
			- Mwall: 5-10
			- Energy bomb: 5-10
			- Fire bomb: 3-5
			- Poison bomb: 3-5
			- Wall runes: 2-3
			- HMM & Icicle: 1
			- Paralyze: 15-30 + Mana cost
			- Soulfire: 15-50

			Potions:
			- Mana potion (30-50): 1
			- Strong mana potion (50-70): 2
			- Great mana potion (70-90): 3
			- Ultimate mana potion (90-110): 4
			- No spirit or life potions
		
			Amulets & Rings:
			Amulets & Rings will automatically de-equip into your bag after expending all charges.
			They will automatically regain all charges when de-equipped.
			The charges will still require soul points.
			- Stone Skin Amulet: 3 per charge
			- Might ring: 1 per charge
			- Time, skill, energy and other rings will be infinite, no soul point usage.

			Ammo:
			- Bolt: Free
			- Arrow: Free
			- Power bolt: 1 Soul
			- Burst arrow: 10-20 mana or 1 Soul
			- Other minor ammo types such as spears, throwing stars will be infinite and free.


	Limited world access:

		Mainland will always be available.
		The rest of the world (such as Ankrahmun, Darashia, Edron etc.) will be available on a daily rotation.
		For example one day Edron is available, next day Ankrahmun & Darashia continent.
		Biggest technical hurdle is teleporting players back to the mainland if the location they are on is no longer available.
		Simplest technical solution is teleporting everyone to Temple after server save. Would love to avoid this solution.

	Spells and formulas:
		Rune and magic formulas would be based on 7.6 system (meaning exp level has a large influence on the damage dealt)
		Certain spells would be adjusted.

		Sorcerer:
		- Great energy beam would have an increased damage range due to the skill required. Also feeds into the fantasy of a sorcerer slinging waves and beams left and right instead of just spamming SDs.
		- Ultimate explosion would be physical, same as SDs.

		Druid:
		- Druid would not have waves, beams or UE.
		- Heal friend (Exura Sio) would be on a lower exhaust, somewhere around 700ms (compared to the regular 1000ms healing exhaust). Reason is druids need to have some sort of advantage over another vocation else just spamming UHs.
		- Mass heal would have a bigger radius.

		Knight:
		- Knights would have a cheap Exori (80-100 mana)
		- Exori gran would be x2 stronger, but would cost 3 times the mana compared to regular exori.
		- No Charge (Utani tempo hur). Utani hur will be the fastest haste spell for knights.

		Paladin:
		- Paladins will receive a new field spell that they conjure under themselves.
		- This new spell will function as a trap, triggering when walked over, creating a small 3x3 radius where everyone affected gets damaged and paralyzed (around 20-50% strength of regular Druid paralyze)



	Limitless party EXP sharing:
		Experience sharing will be automatically turned on in party.
		For each active member in the party, you get a 20% exp bonus - regardless of vocation.
		This would make it possible to create large hunting groups of 10+ people.
		Any player not in range for experience sharing, will not be eligible for sharing.
		The level range for experience sharing will be more relaxed

		Regular tibia uses this formula:
			Lower level limit: ceil(level / 1.5)
			Upper level limit: floor(level * 1.5)

		We will use:
			Lower level limit: ceil(level / 2)
			Upper level limit: floor(level * 2)

		Means a level 50 character would be able to share experience with players level 25-100


	Rebalanced Vocation stats:
		Knight:
		- Double attack speed (once every second)

		Druid:
		- Same weapon and shield advancement as Knight.
		- 7 Health per level
		- 20 mana per level
